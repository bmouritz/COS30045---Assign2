Australia's GDP vs Waste Output

For this visualisation we investigate the waste outputs/waste & GDP over years in Australia.
Here, as explained on the cover page, we wanted to investigate if there was a correlation between increasing GDP and higher waste outputs.
This shows Australia's GDP taken from The National Australian Accounts Report.
In addition to this, we have also used the dataset from Australian Waste Output report from the Australian Government Website as the data source for this visual.
The combination of both of these datasets allows us to see at an Australian level, if there is a correlation between the two variables, GDP & waste output.
We have the years on the X axis, Amount of waste on the left Y axis, and GDP on the right Y axis.
The visualisation has interactivity via the tooltips which show specific numbers when hovering over a datum point.

Green represents the GDP (right y axis)

Red represents the waste output (left y axis)
