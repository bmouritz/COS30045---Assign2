World GDP vs Waste Output

This visualisation seeks to explore the relationship between GDP and waste output per country.
The slider allows you to choose the year and the dropdown allows you to choose the metric that you wish to display on the Choropleth.
The GDP data retrieved from Our World in Data offers the green metric.
The Waste output data retrieved from The UN offers the orange metric.
The Ratio is derived by dividing the GDP by the Waste output of a Country to determine "For each tonne of waste the country produces this much gross product". Hovering over a country will highlight it against the others and clicking on a country will offer an informative tooltip that displays Country, year and metric amount.
