State Waste Output

For this visualisation we investigate the waste outputs/waste output types over years by Australian States/Territories.
Here we wanted to see both the breakdown of types of waste, and the change of the output over time.
By using the Australian Waste Output report from the Australian Government Website as the data source for this visual, we were able to visualise accurate data.
We have the States/Territories on the X axis, and Amount of waste on the Y axis.
The two dropdowns can be used to interact with the data and view different years and/or waste types in depth.
In addition to this tooltips are available for specific numbers when hovering over a State/Territory.
